Caitlin & Geoffrey

WinterWonderGrass Tahoe, March 2015

OUR STORY

Cut to late 2014. Geoffrey's working overnights at Wallaby Yogurt and Caitlin's working for a short-lived music festival production company in an industrial park down the street. Tinder thought we'd be a good match and we agreed. After years of bluegrass festivals and camping trips, a cross-country move, and more craft beer than anybody should reasonably drink, we're tying the knot!



Smuggler's Notch, October 2018

THE ENGAGEMENT

We made the questionable decision to camp in northern Vermont in the middle of October. Geoffrey intended to propose on the top of one of the highest peaks in the state, but the weather had other ideas. In the pouring, freezing rain, by a rushing creek and forest floor littered with leaves in every color imaginable, he got down on one knee. It was perfect.
