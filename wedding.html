Our Wedding Weekend

THE VENUE

We rented a summer camp! Spend the weekend with us on a stunning 200-acre lake in New Hampshire kayaking, playing kickball, and roasting marshmallows around the campfire. The camp is approximately 1 hour and 45 minutes from Boston. Harbor Camps is an incredible non-profit that you can read more about here and we are thrilled that our wedding can support this work. Because of the populations the camp works with, we won't be posting the address online -- keep an eye out for the invitations for more specific details.
