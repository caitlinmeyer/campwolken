Our Wedding Weekend

THE VENUE

We rented a summer camp! Spend the weekend with us on a stunning 200-acre lake in New Hampshire kayaking, playing kickball, and roasting marshmallows around the campfire. The camp is approximately 1 hour and 45 minutes from Boston. Harbor Camps is an incredible non-profit that you can read more about here and we are thrilled that our wedding can support this work. Because of the populations the camp works with, we won't be posting the address online -- keep an eye out for the invitations for more specific details.


ACCOMMODATIONS

Stay on-site and spend the whole weekend with us! The camp has dozens of cabins and brand new shower houses. For the less outdoors-inclined, we are looking into some off-site block options based on interest.



GETTING TO CAMP

Closest airports:

  • Manchester-Boston Regional Airport in Manchester, NH (MHT): Approximately 45 minutes from camp
  • Boston Logan International Airport (BOS): <2 hours without traffic, 2-3 hours with bad Boston traffic
  • Bradley International Airport in Hartford, CT (BDL): 2-2.5 hours, won't be trafficky for the most part
  • TF Green Airport in Providence, RI (PVD): 2.5 hours
We are looking into renting a van or shuttle to move guests from the Manchester airport to camp. If you're interested in this, let us know.
